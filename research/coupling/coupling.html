Coupling Extinction and Angular Static Light Scattering measurements for the characterization (2D) of soot nanoparticles in a flame

Authors: Littin, M., Yon, J., Lefevre, G., Fuentes, A., & Mazur, M.

Presented: April 2023, European Combustion Meeting (ECM) 2023

Abstract

The implementation of an optical bench allowing the measurement of extinction and angular light scattering at different wavelengths on soot particles from a canonical flame is presented. The coupling of these optical techniques associated with the analytical theory of the RDF-FA allows an estimation of different measurands such as volume fraction, temperature and size of aggregates. A strategy for the determination of optical index based on the consideration of amorphous, graphitic and organic compounds is also presented by coupling both techniques at different wavelengths.
